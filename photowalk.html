Bombay

Project Brief

Role

  • Photographer

These photos were taken during a photowalk through Bandra, Fort, and Bora Bazar. Each frame shows a part of Mumbai’s everyday rhythm, where movement and stillness meet in unexpected ways. The focus wasn’t on creating perfect images, but on noticing how the city carries its own character in every corner.

Client

Personal exploration

With

Solo

I was drawn to places that felt familiar yet different every time I passed them. The markets, narrow lanes, and quiet corners all had their own rhythm. I tried to show that contrast through framing and light, how noise and stillness can exist together in one frame. Most of these moments weren’t planned, they just happened while walking, observing, and waiting for the right pause in the flow of the street.
