Project Brief

Role

  • Visual Designer

The project for Personal Fitness Trainer Shashwat Bose was to create a brand identity communicating elite strength and discipline without feeling overly aggressive. The goal was to develop a business card that was distinct, sophisticated, and immediately trustworthy, appealing to clients seeking a high-caliber, personalized fitness experience.

Client

Shashwat Bose

With

Solo

We took inspiration from the idealized strength of classical Greek statuary, translating this heritage into a clean, modern framework. The custom-designed illustration, featuring a classic figure holding a barbell, was treated as a stylized engraving. This core element, combined with a highly disciplined, clean layout and ample negative space, ensured the card felt premium and easy to read.
