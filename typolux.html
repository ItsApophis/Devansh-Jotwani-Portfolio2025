Typolux
Font Design

Project Brief

Role

  • Visual Designer
  • Type Designer
  • Researcher

This project traces the process of researching, sketching, and building a typeface that bridges the long-standing divide between artistry and readability. It began with a detailed analysis of the font industry and competitor offerings, which showed that most decorative fonts succeed only as eye candy while neutral sans serifs read well but fail to leave an impression.

Client

Personal exploration

With

Solo

From there I sketched letters and symbols, tested proportions, refined spacing, and learned the technical workflow of turning drawings into a working font file. Each stage brought feedback and revisions until the typeface struck a balance of expressive character and dependable clarity. The result is Typolux, a font designed to stand out in headlines while staying legible in extended use.
