Project Brief

Role

This project outlines the creation of "Typo-Cards!", a font guessing game designed to be fun and educational for anyone interested in typography. The game deck will feature specific letters from famous typefaces, requiring players to guess the font based on concise hints about its designer, style, and unique parts. The visual design is inspired by bright neon caution tape, giving the card backs a bold, high-contrast, and modern look.

Client

Personal exploration

With

Solo

The project also includes developing a simple manual to explain the game rules and basic font terminology. Custom packaging will be designed to match the striking caution tape aesthetic of the cards, making the entire product visually appealing and consistent. The overall goal is to turn complex font anatomy into an engaging, accessible, and well-designed competitive game.
